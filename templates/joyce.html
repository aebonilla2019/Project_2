Analysis of Medal Counts Through Time

As a proof of concept for our visualizations, we decided to visualize the Olympic Medal results from 1886 to 2016. We decided on this dataset because of its breadth and depth which would allow us to visualize the data with different approaches.

The dataset presented some unique challenges such as omiting the latitude and longitude of countries and including countries that no longer exist or have been renamed.

We have included 3 visualisations:

1. Stacked Bar Chart of Medal Count by year

2. Medal Count of Nations Through Time

3. World Map of Olympic Medal Count
